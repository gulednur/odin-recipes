Dark Chocolate Mocha

Description:

The taste of dark chocolate!

Ingredients:
  • almond milk
  • dark cocoa powder
  • stevia sweetener
  • vanilla extract
  • coffee
Steps:
  1. 1/2 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  2. 2 tablespoons of dark cocoa powder
  3. 1 gram of stevia sweetener
  4. 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  5. 1 cup of strong brewed coffee
